⚔️ Paranoia Duel β — Rules

Local dice game for 2 players. First to the finish wins.

Basics
🎲Each player rolls 1D6 per turn
1️⃣Roll 1 → Death moves near the leader
💀Death on your square → back to start (absolute priority)
🟡Yellow dot → play again immediately
🟢Green dot → swap positions
🔵/🟠Portal → teleports to the other portal (one way)
Special tiles (one use)
💣Bomb — go back 1–6 squares
🎁Gift — next roll is 1D20!
🚀Missile — foe goes back 1–6
🌀Vortex — everyone scattered
Shop 💰 (during your turn)
🛡️ 15💰Amulet — cancels next death (this turn only)
⚡ 10💰Turbo — next roll is 1D12
💣 20💰Place a bomb on the board
